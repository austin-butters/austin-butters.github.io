About Me

Hi, I'm Austin Butters. I live in Wellington, New Zealand, and I'm a student at DevAcademy.

I'm eighteen years old, and I've just graduated from high school. I started working my first job at KFC when I was 16, and continued working there until I quit in March 2025.

I was initially planning on taking a full gap year before going to university in 2026, but quickly realized my whole year would be spent frying chicken, and I didn't see any opportunities for major improvement in the fast food industry (what a surprise!) DevAcademy seemed like the perfect use of 2025 for me, so I'm really glad to be here and learning with everyone.

This blog is one of my first projects, and will act as a record of my learning throughout foundations. Head to my blog page to see what I've been up to!
